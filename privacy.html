Cavault Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2025

Our Commitment

Cavault is built on a “data-never-leaves-your-device” principle. We do not—and will never—collect, transmit, or store your sensitive information on any server.

We Store Nothing

All card details, PINs, passwords, and bank details are encrypted and kept exclusively in Apple’s Keychain. Only non-sensitive labels (e.g. name: “My Visa ****1234”) are stored in app storage locally.

How We Protect It

Apple’s Keychain provides hardware‑level encryption. Every piece of data is encrypted at rest, and only unlocked when you authenticate via Face ID or your passcode.

No Third‑Party & No Tracking

Cavault does not include any analytics, ads, or tracking libraries. We don’t use Crashlytics, Google Analytics, or any SDK that collects usage data. Your activity is 100% private.

Offline‑First & No Backups

The app functions fully offline—no internet connection is required. We do not support any backups as of now for extreme protection if requested will be supported future on optional basis.

You Control Everything

You decide what stays on your device. You can delete individual entries or wipe the entire vault at any time—and all encrypted data will be permanently removed.

Questions & Support

If you have any concerns, email us at support@cavault.app. We respond promptly and transparently.
