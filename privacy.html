Cavault Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2025

1. Data Storage

Cavault is an offline-first app. Your sensitive information (such as card numbers, CVVs, PINs, and passwords) is securely stored only on your device using Apple’s Keychain. No sensitive data is uploaded to the cloud or shared externally.

2. No Internet Access

Cavault does not require or use internet access to function. Your data stays entirely within your device.

3. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks. Your activity in the app is entirely private and not monitored or recorded.

4. Backups

If you want to back up your data, we recommend using encrypted local backups of your iPhone via Finder (macOS) or iTunes (Windows).

5. Permissions

Cavault may ask for access to your device’s biometric authentication (Face ID or Touch ID) to ensure secure access, but this information is never stored or transmitted.

6. Support

If you have questions or need help, you can reach us at support@cavault.app.
