Cavault Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2025

Data Storage

Cavault stores sensitive data (cards, PINs, passwords) only on your device using Apple’s Keychain. No sensitive data is ever sent to the internet.

No Internet

The app is fully offline—your data never leaves your device.

No Tracking

We don’t collect analytics or use any tracking frameworks. Your usage stays private.

Backups

To back up, use encrypted local iPhone backups via Finder (macOS) or iTunes (Windows).

Permissions

Face ID/Touch ID is used only locally for unlocking; we don’t store or transmit biometric data.

Support

Questions? Email support@cavault.app.
